Reservation Process

Thoughtful matching.
Always personal.

PURRFUME operates on a waitlist system — not first-come-first-served, but careful, considered matching between each family and kitten.

1
Enquire & Connect
DM us on Instagram or send an email. We'll discuss our philosophy, current and upcoming litters, and find the right colour and temperament direction for your family.
2
Join the Waitlist
Once we're a confirmed match, you join the waitlist for your preferred pairing and pay a small reservation deposit to secure your place.
3
Notification & Selection
When a suitable kitten is born and reaches evaluation age, we notify waitlisted families in order and share photos and videos for selection.
⚠ Kittens are assessed and confirmed around 3–4 months of age — early stages are not indicative of final appearance.
4
Contract & Collection
Once health checks and vaccinations are complete, we sign the contract, finalise payment, and arrange delivery. Your kitten travels safely to your nearest international airport.

Health Guarantee

We stand behind
every kitten.

Under the premise of veterinary diagnosis at a licensed animal hospital, PURRFUME will responsibly and compassionately address the following conditions.

01
Feline Panleukopenia
FPV
If a kitten is diagnosed with FPV within one year of completing the final first-year vaccine at a licensed hospital, we provide a full refund or a replacement kitten of equivalent quality.
02
Feline Infectious Peritonitis
FIP
If a kitten is diagnosed with FIP and passes away within 30 days of arriving at their new home, we provide a full refund or a replacement kitten of equivalent quality.
03
Hereditary Conditions
PKD · PRA · Genetic Disease
Clearly detectable hereditary conditions — including Polycystic Kidney Disease (PKD), Progressive Retinal Atrophy (PRA), and other serious genetically confirmed issues — are covered upon provision of test reports.
All guarantees require: licensed veterinary diagnosis · reasonable care conditions · mutual communication and confirmation
